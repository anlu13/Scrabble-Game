Instructions:

Simple Scrabble is a simplified version of Scrabble. As a player, your goal is to use the given letter tiles, place them onto the game board to create words, and score as many points as possible.

Valid words must be a single word (no empty spaces between letters), at least 2 letters or more, and found within the game's internal dictionary of over 73k words.

The scoreboard in contained within the black outlined box.

  • "Word" shows the current word the player has placed onto the board. The Â· character indicates an empty space between tiles.
  • "Score" displays the current score the player has achieved in this round.
  • "Remaining Tiles" shows the current number of tiles left in reserve.
  • "Highest Score" displays the highest total score earned through all consecutive rounds played.
